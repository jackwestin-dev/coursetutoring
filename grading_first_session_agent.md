# SOP: 515+/Intensive Session Grading Rubric (135-Point Architecture)

## Purpose
This SOP defines how the grading agent evaluates 515+/Intensive tutoring sessions (Sessions 1-3) using a **135-point scoring architecture** across 4 categories: **SOP Compliance (50 pts)**, **Coaching Quality (50 pts)**, **Notes & Documentation (20 pts)**, and **Professionalism (15 pts)**.

The goal is to assess **process adherence, instructional effectiveness, documentation quality, and professional conduct** with SOP and Coaching as co-dominant weights (37% each).

---

## Scoring Overview

| Category | What It Grades | Points | % of Total |
|----------|----------------|--------|------------|
| A. SOP Compliance | Required session deliverables and process adherence | 50 pts | 37% |
| B. Coaching Quality | In-session teaching behavior from transcript | 50 pts | 37% |
| C. Notes & Documentation | Quality of tutor's written notes | 20 pts | 15% |
| D. Professionalism | Professional conduct and communication | 15 pts | 11% |
| **TOTAL** | | **135 pts** | 100% |

**Grade Scale:**
- 120–135 → **Excellent** (89–100%)
- 100–119 → **Satisfactory** (74–88%)
- 80–99 → **Needs Improvement** (59–73%)
- Below 80 → **Unsatisfactory** (<59%)

---

## Category A: SOP Compliance — 50 Points

### A1. Session Structure & Timing (10 pts)
- Follows SOP time blocks (intro, strategy, wrap-up) — 4 pts [binary]
- Covers all required agenda items for that session — 4 pts [binary]
- Doesn't skip or rush wrap-up section — 2 pts [binary]

### A2. Study Schedule & Exam Planning (14 pts)
- Study schedule created in Google Sheet — 4 pts [binary]
- 10 full-length exams scheduled with real dates — 6 pts [proportional credit allowed]
- AAMC question packs assigned (if student has them) — 4 pts [binary, auto full credit if student doesn't have them]

**Full-Length Exams (mandatory, 6 pts):**
Tutors must schedule 10 full-length practice exams. Sources: Jack Westin Full Length 1–6 and AAMC exams. Detection keywords: "exam", "full length", "JW FL", "AAMC exam".

**CRITICAL — Default/Placeholder Date:** The student notes sheet has a default date of **"March 5"** (or "Mar 5", "3/5", "03/05") pre-filled. Exams with March 5th do NOT count as scheduled. Only exams with dates other than March 5th are valid. If all/most exams show March 5th, award zero.

**AAMC Question Packs/Resources (conditional, 4 pts):**
These are SEPARATE from exams. The 10 AAMC resources:
1. MCAT Biology Question Pack, Volume 1 & 2
2. MCAT Chemistry Question Pack
3. MCAT Physics Question Pack
4. MCAT CARS Question Pack, Volume 1 & 2
5. MCAT Section Bank
6. MCAT Official Prep Hub Independent Question Bank
7. MCAT Official Prep CARS Diagnostic Tool
8. MCAT Official Prep Flashcards

Only required IF the student has these resources. Auto full credit if student has none.

### A3. Pre-Session & Post-Session Tasks (12 pts)
- Pre-session notes completed before session — 3 pts [binary]
- Student overview/survey data reviewed — 3 pts [binary]
- Post-session: in-session notes completed — 3 pts [binary]
- Post-session: notes shared with directors — 3 pts [binary]

### A4. Session-Specific Requirements (14 pts)
Only evaluate items for the actual session number:
- **S1:** Booking link shared (3 pts) + 2+ strategy videos recommended (3 pts) = 6 pts max
- **S2:** FL review process coached (4 pts) = 4 pts max
- **S3:** Test-day toolkit verbalized by student (4 pts) = 4 pts max

Remaining points not applicable to the session are not scored — do NOT penalize.

---

## Category B: Coaching Quality — 50 Points

Evaluated from the **transcript**, not the notes.

### B1. Socratic Method & Guided Questioning (15 pts)
- Uses probing questions vs. lecturing — 5 pts
- Student does most of the talking — 5 pts
- Doesn't re-map passages for the student — 5 pts

### B2. Weakness Identification & Feedback (15 pts)
- Clearly identifies missed question reasons — 5 pts
- Doesn't let excuses slide — 5 pts
- Provides actionable, specific strategies — 5 pts

### B3. Passage Practice Execution (10 pts)
- 3+ questions reviewed together — 4 pts
- Student teaches passage back (not tutor) — 3 pts
- Constructive feedback ~once per paragraph — 3 pts

### B4. Student Engagement & Takeaways (10 pts)
- Asks student for main takeaway(s) — 4 pts
- Asks if student has questions/concerns — 3 pts
- Addresses timing AND accuracy (not just one) — 3 pts

**Positive signals:** "What do you think?" / "Why is that?" / "How would you approach this?" / hint then wait / student explains back / "Does that make sense?" with follow-up.

**Negative signals:** Tutor answers own questions; long monologues (>2 min) without student engagement; tutor gives answer before student responds; only surface confirmations ("got it?", "okay?").

---

## Category C: Notes & Documentation — 20 Points

- Student notes template properly named and linked — 3 pts
- Student Overview tab completed — 3 pts
- In-session notes are detailed and specific — 5 pts
- Exam Progress tab updated — 3 pts
- Next steps clearly written (not just verbal) — 3 pts
- Added to Activity Completion Tracking (Col M) — 3 pts

---

## Category D: Professionalism — 15 Points

- Calm, confident, supportive demeanor — 3 pts
- Doesn't guarantee outcomes or promise scores — 3 pts
- Refers students to proper channels (not pricing) — 3 pts
- Session starts/ends on time — 3 pts
- Communication on approved platforms only — 3 pts

---

## Scoring Rules

- Most items are **BINARY**: full points or zero. Did it or didn't.
- Partial credit ONLY where explicitly noted (e.g., FL exam scheduling = proportional)
- Three sources of truth: transcript, student notes document, and manual SOP verification inputs
- If ANY source confirms an item, give credit
- SOP verification inputs: Yes = full points, Partial = 50%, No = 0 (unless other sources confirm)
- March 5 dates are placeholders — do NOT count as real scheduled dates
- AAMC question packs: auto full credit if student doesn't have them

---

## Final Evaluation Output

Agent should return:
- **A total (0–50)** with per-item scores and evidence
- **B total (0–50)** with per-item scores and evidence
- **C total (0–20)** with per-item scores and evidence
- **D total (0–15)** with per-item scores and evidence
- **Raw total (0–135)** and **Overall rating**
- Per-section justifications and missing items; tutor feedback

---

## Agent Guidance
- Score items as binary unless partial credit is explicitly noted.
- When uncertain, score conservatively (toward zero).
- Always check transcript for takeaways in the **last 20%** of the session.
- **Full-Length Exams vs. AAMC Question Packs:** Scored SEPARATELY. "AAMC exam" = full-length exam (A2). "AAMC question pack/section bank/flashcards" = AAMC resources (A2).
- **AAMC conditional rule:** If student doesn't have AAMC resources, award full credit automatically.
- **Dual-source rule:** Evidence can come from EITHER the transcript OR the student notes. Both are valid.
- **SOP Verification Inputs:** Third source of truth. YES = full credit, PARTIAL = 50%, NO = 0 unless other sources confirm.
- **Session-specific A4:** Only score items for the actual session number being graded.

---

## Core Principle
This rubric evaluates **SOP compliance, coaching quality, documentation thoroughness, and professional conduct** using binary scoring for accountability and clarity.
